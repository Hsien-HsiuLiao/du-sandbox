HSIEN-HSIU - GUITAR & SYNTH

"I've taken it upon myself to kind of use this as a launching pad to my own personal web page. What you will see is a documentary/journal of my thoughts on the whole music thing. Hopefully those of you who read it will get an idea of the Austin metal scene and how one changes with the band. What I also hope to accomplish is to have those that have read it come away knowing something a little more than they normally would with a band. I think it's important for fans to have that type of connection with the band. Enjoy" --Hsien-Hsiu
